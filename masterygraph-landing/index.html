Frequently Asked Questions

What age range is OBIO for?

OBIO covers ages 4-11 (pre-K through 5th grade). The Marble Skill Taxonomy includes 1,590 topics across math, literacy, science, and more — all calibrated to age-appropriate learning.

How is this different from Khan Academy or IXL?

Those platforms use linear skill trees. OBIO uses a real dependency graph with 3,221 prerequisite relationships. When your child struggles, we trace backwards through the graph to find the exact missing foundation — not just "try easier problems."

Does it align with school standards?

Yes. OBIO maps to 7 curricula including CCSS (Common Core), NGSS (Science), and IB. You can generate standards alignment reports for homeschool documentation or parent-teacher meetings.

Can I use it for homeschooling?

Absolutely. OBIO was designed with homeschool parents in mind. Create personalized plans, track mastery across subjects, and generate compliance reports — all from one dashboard.

What happens to my data?

Your child's learning data is private and encrypted. We never sell data. You can export or delete everything at any time. We're parents too — we take this seriously.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. The Free plan includes 1 learner profile with full access to topic search, path computation, and gap analysis. Upgrade to Family or Educator when you need more learners or advanced features.
