3. Full OBIO Brand Guidelines

A. Brand Soul (Voice & Tone)

· Archetype: The Brilliant, Gentle Mentor. OBIO is not a toy, nor a strict teacher. It's the quiet genius in the room that never makes a child feel dumb.
· To Parents: Confident, data-clear, reassuring. ("We don't guess. We map.")
· To Children: Calm, encouraging, never punitive. OBIO says "Not yet!" instead of "Wrong."
· Signature Phrase: "OBIO sees your spark."

B. Color Palette (Cyber-Warm)

· Primary (The Engine): Deep Teal (#0F766E) – intelligence, focus, depth.
· Secondary (The Code): Dark Indigo (#312E81) – structured knowledge graph.
· Accent (The Spark): Neon Amber (#F59E0B) – the child's unique light, used for CTAs, progress nodes, and the hidden heart.
· Background: Dark Mode #1 (#0B1120) for the app (makes it feel premium/tech) or Warm Off-White (#F8F6F2) for the website.
· Typography: Headlines = Inter (Bold, ultra-crisp). Body = Inter (Regular). Child UI = Quicksand (soft).

C. Logo Rules

· Primary Lockup: The word OBIO in heavy, geometric sans-serif (Inter Bold, tracked out). The "O" is not a circle—it's a hexagonal node with a single glowing amber dot at its center (representing the child's heart).
· Sub-text: "by ObiomaCare" in tiny, clean caps beneath (only on the website footer/loading screen).
· Minimum Size: 40px height for web.
· Do Not: Ever spell out "ObiomaCare" as the primary logo. OBIO is king.

Logo Prompt (for Midjourney/Flux):

Minimal, ultra-modern tech logo for "OBIO". Bold geometric sans-serif lettering in deep teal. The letter "O" is a dark hexagonal node with a single glowing warm gold dot at its exact center. Clean, futuristic, educational, trustworthy. No extra ornaments. White background, high contrast, vector style.

5. Parent Onboarding Flow (The "Activation" Sequence)

Goal: From signup to first lesson in under 3 minutes. Make it feel like setting up a smart device.

· Step 0: Parent clicks "Activate OBIO" on the hero.
· Step 1: Quick Sign-up – Email + Password (or Google/Apple).
· Step 2: The "Why" Question – "What does your child need most?" (Buttons: "Catching up", "Staying ahead", "Building confidence", "Homeschool support").
· Step 3: Child Profile – Name + Age/Grade. "OBIO learns who they are first."
· Step 4: The 5-Minute "Spark Scan" – A bright, game-like diagnostic. No "fail" states—just levels that say "OBIO is still discovering you!"
· Step 5: The Reveal (WOW moment) – The dashboard loads. A beautiful constellation map appears. Teal nodes = mastered. Amber glowing nodes = the next 3 steps. A tiny animation plays: "OBIO found your spark. Here's where you start."
· Step 6: Commitment – "How much time daily?" (15/30/45 mins). Set a smart reminder.
· Final Step: Redirect to app.obiomacare.com/child-dashboard with a soft chime and a message: "Let the decoding begin."

6. API Documentation Structure (for docs.obiomacare.com)

Built with Swagger/OpenAPI.

· Overview
  · Base URL: https://api.obiomacare.com/v1/
  · Auth: Bearer API Key (get from your OBIO dashboard).
  · Rate Limits: 1,000/day (Free), 10,000/day (Pro).
· Core Resources:
  1. Children – CRUD operations for learner profiles.
  2. Spark Scan – Initiate diagnostic, fetch results, get mastery matrix.
  3. Paths – Fetch prerequisite-ordered lesson sequences for any skill.
  4. Activities – Fetch content by node ID, mark completion, sync external progress.
  5. Progress – Historical mastery trends, time-spent analytics, growth scores.
· Example Payloads: Full JSON request/response for each endpoint.
· Webhooks: Milestone unlocked, Path completed, Weekly progress summary.
