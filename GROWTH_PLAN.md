## Growth Engines

### Engine 1: Free Assessment Tool (Viral Acquisition)
- Create a "Free Learning Readiness Assessment" for any topic
- Parents enter child's age + topic → get personalized prerequisite report
- Report is shareable, branded with MasteryGraph
- CTA: "See full learning path → Upgrade to Family Plan"
- Target: 5,000 assessments in Month 1, 50,000 by Month 6

### Engine 2: Content/SEO (Organic Traffic)
- Publish 2 educational guides per week (prerequisite paths, learning tips)
- Target: "how to teach [topic] to [age] year old" keywords
- Each guide links to free assessment tool
- Target: 10,000 organic visitors/month by Month 6

### Engine 3: Partnerships (B2B Sales)
- Homeschool networks (co-ops, associations)
- Tutoring centers (Kumon, Mathnasium, independent)
- Micro-schools and pods
- Parent Facebook groups (target 100 groups)
- Offer: 20% commission on referrals, white-label reports

### Engine 4: Email Nurture (Conversion)
- 7-day email sequence for free assessment users
- Weekly newsletter: "This Week's Learning Insight"
- Case study emails: "How [Parent] helped their child master [topic]"
- Target: 8% free-to-paid conversion

### Engine 5: Referral/Viral Loop
- "Share your child's learning path — get 1 month free"
- Referral dashboard in app
- Social sharing cards: "My 7-year-old is mastering [topic]!"
- Target: 0.3 viral coefficient (each user brings 0.3 new users)
